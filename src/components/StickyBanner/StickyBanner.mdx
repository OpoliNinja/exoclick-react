# Sticky Banner

A display banner that can be made to "stick" in a specific position on a desktop screen, 9 positions are available.
The sticky banner comes in various sizes: 160x600, 300x250, 728x90 and 900x250.

## Basic usage
<Playground>
  <StickyBanner zoneId="4294580" horizontalPosition="right" verticalPosition="middle" format="160x600" />
</Playground>
